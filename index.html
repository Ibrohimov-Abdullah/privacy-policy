Privacy Policy — Mening Hamshiram Effective Date: April 23, 2026 Last Updated: April 23, 2026 1. Introduction Welcome to Mening Hamshiram ("the App", "we", "our", or "us"). Mening Hamshiram is an on-demand home nursing service application that connects patients with professional nurses in the Andijan region of Uzbekistan. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. 2. Information We Collect 2.1 Information You Provide Directly - Phone number — used for account registration and authentication (OTP verification) - Full name — to identify your account and display to the assigned nurse - Patient information — age and gender, provided when placing an order, used to match you with an appropriate nurse - Delivery address — the address where you want the nurse to arrive, including street, building, entrance, and apartment number - Order details — services selected, schedule preferences, and additional comments - Complaints and feedback — if you submit a complaint or feedback through the App 2.2 Information Collected Automatically - Location (GPS) — your real-time geographic location is used to show your position on the map and determine where the nurse should go. Location is only accessed while using the App. - Device token (FCM) — a Firebase Cloud Messaging token is collected to send you push notifications about your order status - Order history — records of your past orders stored in our database 2.3 Payment Information - We support Cash payments and Click (Uzbekistan online payment system) payments - We do not collect or store your bank card details directly. Card processing is handled entirely by Click — please refer to [Click's Privacy Policy](https://click.uz) for their data practices 3. How We Use Your Information We use the information we collect to: - Create and manage your account - Verify your identity via OTP (one-time password) - Process and manage your nurse orders - Show your location on the map so the nurse can find you - Send push notifications about your order status (e.g., nurse accepted, nurse en route, completed) - Process payments through Click - Respond to complaints and improve the service - Ensure the safety and security of the platform 4.
